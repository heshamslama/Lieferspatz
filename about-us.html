About LieferSpatz

Founded in 2024, LieferSpatz is a leading online food delivery platform. Our mission is to provide a seamless, reliable, and enjoyable food delivery experience for customers and restaurant partners alike.

Our Vision

We envision a world where ordering food is as easy and enjoyable as possible, with fast, reliable delivery and a wide variety of restaurants to choose from. We aim to be the go-to platform for food lovers everywhere.

Our History

LieferSpatz was founded by a passionate team of food enthusiasts, tech experts, and entrepreneurs. We started with a simple idea: to make food delivery more accessible, reliable, and efficient. Since then, we've grown rapidly and expanded our services to multiple cities.
